How asthmaGuard works

1

Connect your smartwatch or smartphone

Link your Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Samsung, WearOS device, or smartphone and allow access to health data like heart rate, activity levels, and sleep patterns. Works on both iPhone and Android.

2

Log your symptoms

Record asthma symptoms and attacks in the app whenever they occur. This helps build your personal health profile.

3

Get AI-powered insights

Our AI models, combined with insights from the latest research in respiratory health, learn your unique patterns and alert you when risk levels rise, giving you time to take preventive action.
